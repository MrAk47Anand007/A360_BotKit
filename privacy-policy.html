Privacy Policy for Bot Logging Enhancer

Last updated: October 7, 2025

Summary: Bot Logging Enhancer does not collect, store, or transmit any personal user data. All operations are performed locally in your browser.

1. Introduction

Welcome to Bot Logging Enhancer ("we," "our," or "the extension"). This Chrome extension is developed by Anand S Kale to enhance the functionality of Automation Anywhere A360 bots by providing utilities to improve bot logging, including the ability to add line numbers to log messages.

We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our extension operates and confirms that we do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information.

2. Information Collection and Use

2.1 No Personal Data Collection

Bot Logging Enhancer does not collect any personal information from users. This includes but is not limited to:

  • Names, email addresses, or contact information
  • IP addresses or location data
  • Browsing history or search queries
  • Cookies or tracking identifiers
  • User credentials or authentication tokens (see section 2.3 for details)

2.2 Local Processing Only

All functionality of the Bot Logging Enhancer extension operates entirely within your local browser environment. The extension:

  • Processes bot content locally in your browser
  • Performs line number calculations on your device
  • Updates bot logging structures without sending data to external servers
  • Stores temporary data only in your browser's local storage (if needed)

2.3 Authentication Token Handling

The extension accesses your Automation Anywhere A360 authentication token stored in your browser's local storage to communicate with the Automation Anywhere Control Room API. Important points:

  • The authentication token is only accessed from your browser's local storage
  • The token is used solely to make API requests to your Automation Anywhere Control Room
  • The token is never transmitted to any third-party servers
  • The token is not stored by the extension beyond the active session
  • All API communications occur directly between your browser and your Automation Anywhere Control Room

3. Permissions Explanation

The extension requests the following Chrome permissions for its core functionality:

3.1 activeTab Permission

Allows the extension to interact with the currently active tab to access bot content from the Automation Anywhere A360 bot editor.

3.2 storage Permission

Used to temporarily store extension settings and preferences locally in your browser. No personal data is stored.

3.3 scripting Permission

Enables the extension to inject content scripts into web pages to interact with the Automation Anywhere A360 interface.

Important: All permissions are used exclusively for the extension's intended functionality and do not enable any form of data collection or tracking.

4. Data Sharing and Third Parties

Bot Logging Enhancer does not share any data with third parties because it does not collect any data. The extension:

  • Does not communicate with any external servers operated by us
  • Does not use analytics or tracking services
  • Does not display advertisements
  • Only communicates with your configured Automation Anywhere Control Room

5. Data Security

Since the extension operates entirely locally and does not collect or transmit personal data, there is minimal security risk. However, we recommend:

  • Keeping your browser and the extension updated to the latest version
  • Using the extension only on trusted devices
  • Following your organization's security policies when working with Automation Anywhere A360

6. Children's Privacy

Our extension is designed for professional use with Automation Anywhere A360 and is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
